## Socks5 ##
**一键安装脚本：**

    bash <(curl https://socks5.v2ray-install.ml/v2ray-install.sh)

**一键卸载脚本：**

    bash <(curl https://socks5.v2ray-install.ml/v2ray-uninstall.sh)

**一键重装脚本：**

    bash <(curl https://socks5.v2ray-install.ml/v2ray-reinstall.sh)
